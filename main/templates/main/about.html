About Mauli Traders

Trusted B2B & B2C Wholesale Distributor for Over a Decade - Delivering Quality Across Marathwada

Our Journey

2010: A humble start in Chh. Sambhaji Nagar, supplying local hardware needs with honesty and dedication.

2015: Expanded operations to nearby towns, building a reputation for reliable wholesale and retail distribution.

2020: Became a direct dealer sourcing from top brands, cutting out middlemen and offering unbeatable pricing.

2023: Now serving 30+ cities and 200+ villages across Marathwada with efficient logistics and broad product variety.

Our Business Model

B2C Sales

B2B Wholesale

Direct Dealership
